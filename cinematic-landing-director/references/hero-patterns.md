# Hero Patterns

## 1. Cinematic Product Reveal

A product or service appears from shadow/light with layered depth, strong typography, and minimal but confident CTA placement.

Use for premium services, repair shops, tech products, agencies, devices.

## 2. X-Ray Service Reveal

The hero visual shows a surface state and an expert-inspection state. Cursor or scroll reveals hidden structure.

Use for repair, auditing, diagnostics, consulting, technical services.

## 3. Mission Control

Central command panel, orbiting issues/services, moving paths, calm control feel.

Use for management, operations, SaaS, property, logistics, support.

## 4. Before/After Tension

Split screen or morphing scene shows pain transforming into clarity, speed, order, or premium result.

Use for redesigns, repair, cleaning, accounting, automation, local services.

## 5. Living Blueprint

Blueprint, map, document, or architecture system becomes alive through scroll, beams, and reveals.

Use for property, construction, legal, admin, planning, B2B services.

## 6. Floating Product Universe

Products/services float in a curated spatial system around the main promise.

Use for e-commerce, devices, productized services, premium shops.

## 7. Premium Editorial Split

Big typography, strong photography/visual asset, luxury pacing, minimal effects.

Use when trust and elegance matter more than flashy interaction.

## 8. Scroll-Driven Transformation

Hero continues into the next sections. Visitor scrolls through a transformation story.

Use when you need Awwwards-style narrative flow.
