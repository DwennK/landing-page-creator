## Component Formula

A strong component = metaphor + user action + business message + conversion purpose.

Examples:

- Device Repair Lens: cursor acts as a diagnostic lens over a damaged phone, revealing the repaired state underneath.
- PPE Control Map: building issues orbit around a calm central management hub and resolve on scroll.
- Trust Path: animated path connects proof points, certifications, reviews, and CTA.
- Service Orbit: services rotate around the product/customer, each expanding on hover with one concrete benefit.
- Timeline Tunnel: visitor scrolls through a process as if moving through a cinematic tunnel.
