# Review Checklist

## Hero

- Is the promise understandable in 3 seconds?
- Is the visual metaphor clear?
- Does the hero feel custom to this company?
- Is the CTA visible and compelling?
- Is there one signature interaction?
- Is the mobile hero still impressive?

## Visual Design

- Does the page have a coherent art direction?
- Is the typography hierarchy strong?
- Is spacing intentional?
- Are shadows, radii, colors, and motion consistent?
- Does the design avoid generic AI patterns?

## Conversion

- Is the audience clear?
- Are the pain points specific?
- Are objections answered?
- Are trust signals visible?
- Is the final CTA strong?

## Code

- Are components clean and reusable?
- Is the page responsive?
- Is accessibility respected?
- Is reduced motion handled?
- Are dependencies justified?
- Did browser preview reveal any obvious issue?
